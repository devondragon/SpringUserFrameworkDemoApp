Password strength:
Password must:
  • Be at least 8 characters
  • Include at least 1 uppercase letter (A–Z)
  • Include at least 1 lowercase letter (a–z)
  • Include at least 1 digit (0–9)
  • Include at least 1 special character:
  • Not be a common or easily guessed password
  • Be different from your last 3 passwords
  • Not be too similar to email
